Our Story

Built in response to a broken system.

We are not trying to be a giant brand. We are trying to prove that a better food system is still possible when somebody is willing to actually build it.

I spent 12 years in the medical and commercial cannabis industry, building clean organic production systems for patients who needed quality they could trust. Then my father was diagnosed with a degenerative brain disease. I sold my company, moved to Florida to take care of him, and started looking at the food on grocery store shelves the same way I used to look at medicine.

What I saw was a system rigged against everyone in it. Farmers squeezed by costs they did not create. Customers paying premium prices for food shipped halfway across the country. Soil destroyed by salt-based nutrients and EDTA chelates that have no place in clean food production. A supply chain bloated with middlemen who add nothing to the product but cost.

TerraVida Organics is what I built in response. Our model is simple. Grow clean food locally using methods refined over a decade in a far more demanding industry. Cut out the long-distance shipping, the unnecessary middlemen, and the chemical inputs that degrade both the land and the product. Sell directly to the chefs, retailers, and families who actually live near the farm.

The cannabis industry taught me two things. Quality is non-negotiable. And keeping overhead low enough to compete is what separates real operations from the ones that fold. TerraVida is built on both.

Restaurant-grade microgreens, grown the right way.

Our Method

A decade of clean growing, brought to your plate.

01

Clean inputs only.

No salt-based nutrients. No EDTA chelates. We grow with our own organic compost tea on hemp mats — the same precision approach refined over twelve years in medical cannabis production.

02

Local first, always.

Direct from our farm to chefs, retailers, and families across Tampa Bay and St. Pete. No long-distance shipping. No middlemen adding cost without adding value. Fresher product, lower prices.

03

Seed to sale traceability.

Every batch is tracked from seeding to harvest. QR codes on our packaging let you watch the actual time-lapse of your microgreens being grown — full transparency, no marketing fluff.
